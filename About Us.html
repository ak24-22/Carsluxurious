Who Are We?

AutoFix Solutions is a reputable and innovative company in the automotive industry, established over two decades ago. Headquartered in Detroit, Michigan, AutoFix Solutions has expanded its presence across the United States, with service centers in over 50 major cities. The company's mission is to provide high-quality, reliable, and affordable automotive repair and maintenance services. With a dedicated team of experienced mechanics and customer service professionals, AutoFix Solutions has earned a reputation for excellence and trust among its customers.

Specializing in comprehensive vehicle repair services, AutoFix Solutions stands out for its expertise in fixing critical components such as wheels, seats, and more. Their skilled technicians are equipped with state-of-the-art tools and technology to handle everything from tire replacements and alignments to complex seat repairs and upholstery work. Whether it's addressing everyday wear and tear or tackling more serious damage caused by accidents, AutoFix Solutions ensures that every vehicle is restored to optimal condition. Their commitment to quality workmanship and attention to detail sets them apart in the competitive automotive repair market.

Beyond wheels and seats, AutoFix Solutions offers a wide range of additional services to meet all automotive needs. This includes engine diagnostics, brake repairs, transmission services, and electrical system troubleshooting. They also provide routine maintenance services like oil changes, battery replacements, and fluid checks to help customers keep their vehicles running smoothly. With a focus on customer satisfaction, AutoFix Solutions offers transparent pricing, timely service, and convenient scheduling options, making them a go-to choice for car owners seeking reliable and efficient vehicle care.
